Special Spawn Points

Lonely House

High-risk, high-reward spawn point with direct line of sight to bridge.
  • Move into the house and lay down in the right room
  • Check for PMCs climbing up the rocky ridge all the way to the right
  • When they are standing 100% still zero scope to 150m and take a shot
