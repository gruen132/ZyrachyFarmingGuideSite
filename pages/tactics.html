Combat Scenarios - Day Time

Note: Zryachiy should ALWAYS be visible in all scenarios

The easiest scenario to master

1. Lay down in the center where you can't be seen by the right guard

2. Set your scope to 200 meters and fire two shots at the top of Zryachiy's head

3. Once Zryachiy is dead, stand up and immediately sprint while going prone to the left for cover

4. At the slowest movement speed, peek through the fence to spot the right guard, adjusting your crouch height one step up

5. Once the right guard is down, navigate through the mines and reach the island

6. Move immediately onto the designated rock mentioned in the video

7. When the left guard moves along his path, unload your entire magazine into either his face or chest

Combat Scenarios - Night Time

Scope ZEROING VALUES
Zryachiy ~450m
Left Guard ~400m
Right Guard ~350m

  1. Walk into the shown area
  2. Zryachiy and his guards will not shoot you there
  3. Walk up to the concrete block shown in the image below and go prone
  4. Make sure you are in cover from Zryachiy and the right guard
  5. Kill the left guard and wait for him to respawn, then crawl back to another position (repeat 4 times)
  6. Once the last left guard has been killed, take cover for 30 seconds to deaggro Zryachiy
  7. Stand up, walk to the 2nd position shown below and go prone
  8. Kill the right guard whilst in cover from Zryachiy
  9. After killing him, stay in cover for 30 seconds and then kill Zryachiy from anywhere
  1. Walk into the shown area
  2. Zryachiy and his guards will not shoot you there
  3. Walk up to the location shown in the image below and go prone
  4. Make sure you are in cover from both guards (left and right)
  5. Kill Zryachiy
  6. Once Zryachiy is killed, take cover for 30 seconds to deaggro the guards
  7. Stand up, walk to the concrete block shown below and go prone
  8. Kill the left guard whilst in cover from the right guard
  9. After killing him, stay in cover for 30 seconds and then kill the right guard from anywhere

Additional Tips:

  • A bipod can be super useful here
  • Bring at least a stack of extra ammo as it might take some tries to hit them correctly
  • The accuracy is drastically decreased due to the large range
  • The "safe" zone is still the same.
  • Once you kill anybody from the group (Zyrachy, GuardLeft, GuardRight), the others will become aggroed and shoot you even in the "safe" zone.
  • When you vanish for about 30 seconds, they will deaggro, and you can peek in the safe zone again.
  • The left guard sometimes tries to go behind cover when he gets hit.
  • He either:
  • 5.1: After some time, goes back to another position on his rock.
  • 5.2: Stays hidden until you go onto the island, where you can peek him like normal again.
  • If Zyrachy is killed first, only 2 guards remain.

NOTE: If any of the guards is not visible, refer to killing them in DAYTIME section.

  • Head and chest hitboxes become blocked
