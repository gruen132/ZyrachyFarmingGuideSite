Introduction to Farming Zryachiy

Last updated: 2025-02-24 00:24:55 UTC Patch: 0.16.1.2

This mission has an extremely high failure rate. Prepare accordingly.

Welcome to your complete guide on farming Zryachiy—one of the most frustrating yet rewarding encounters in Escape from Tarkov. This guide is for both beginners and experienced players, covering multiple strategies and detailed methods to take him down. Be ready for a brutal grind—between game-breaking bugs, overpowered guards, and other players looking to ruin your run, you’re going to die. A lot. But with the right prep and strategy, you can turn this chaotic mess into a profitable farm.

Success Rate 15%
Average Attempt Time 25-30 min

What to Prepare For

  • Frequent Deaths: The path to Zryachiy is unforgiving. Numerous deaths are almost guaranteed—not just from combat, but also due to game bugs and erratic guard behavior.
  • Heavy Financial Losses: From costly gear runs that end in failure to unexpected insurance losses, every attempt will drain your stash and test your ability to sustain repeated setbacks.
  • Huge Profit Potential: Mastering these runs can lead to some of the biggest payouts in Tarkov, letting you secure top-tier loot and stack your stash with high-value gear.
